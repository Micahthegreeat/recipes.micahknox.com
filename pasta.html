Pasta

Recipe donated By: Antonio

Ingredient Amount
pasta 2 box
tomato sauce 2 jars
ground beef 1 lb
bratworst .5-1 lb
block of sharp chedar cheese 2lb

Step 1

get a large pot and fill it with water and salt the water heavily and let come to a boil,

Step 2

pre heat oven to 350 degrees,

Step 3

put the past in water till it is cooked to al dente,

Step 4

cut the skin of the bratwurst and open the ground beef package,

Step 5

a cast iron skillet and pre heat it on a high heat,

Step 6

once cast iron is heated up put the beef and brat in pan and cook it browned throughly,

Step 7

strain the pasta when cooked to aldentay and save some pasta water (1/2-1 cup),

Step 8

shred all of the cheddar cheese,

Step 9

add the tomato sauce to the cast iron (add a bit of the pasta water to the jar to get almost all of the sauce out),

Step 10

start to add the cheese in a small amount at a time until 1/2 of the cheese is mix throughly in,

Step 11

add the pasta water back to the pasta to rehydrate the pasta and put in large pot,

Step 12

ladle pasta sauce into the pot and mix after 2-3 ladles of sauce,

Step 13

grab either a very large oven safe glass dish or multiple small oven safe pans,

Step 14

add a light layers of pasta, cheese, and pasta( save a cup of cheese,

Step 15

place the the the glass oven dish in oven for 15-20 minutes,

Step 16

pull the pasta out, set the oven to broil and add 1 and a half hand fulls of cheese to the top of the pasta and put back into the oven until cheese is melted throughly
