Trail Pudding
Ingredient Amount
instant puding 1/2 package
dry milk powder 1/3 cup
water 1 cup

Step 1

To be able to easly measure out the amount of water you want on the trail put a line 3 and 1/4 inch off the bottom of a normal sandwich bag.

Step 2

Mix the powders in the bag

Step 3

on the trail add water to the line on the bag. It should be about 1 cup of water.
