Cookie Dough
Ingredient Amount
flour 1 and 1/4 cups
butter 1/2 cup
brown sugar 1/2 cup
sugar 1/3 cup
salt 1/4 tsp
half and half(milk) 1 and 1/2 tbsp
vanilla extract 1 tsp
choclate chips 1/2 cup

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Evenly spread flour on a baking pan with parchment paper on it or something. Bake in the oven for 5 minutes. Then, wait for it to cool completly.

Step 2

Cream togother butter, brown sugar, granulated sugar, and salt until pale and fluffy.

Step 3

Stir in milk and flour.

Step 4

Mix in choclate chips.

Step 5

Refridgeate in the shape you want the finished product in.
