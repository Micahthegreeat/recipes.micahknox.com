Cookie Dough Bites
Ingredient Amount
flour 1 and 1/4 cups
butter 1/2 cup
brown sugar 1/2 cup
sugar 1/3 cup
salt 1/4 tsp
half and half(milk) 1 and 1/2 tbsp
vanilla extract 1 tsp
choclate chips 1/2 cup

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Sprinkle flour evenly onto a rimmed 18 by 13 inch baking sheet. bake in preheated oven 5 minutes, then remove and let cool completly.

Step 2

In the bowl of an electric mixer fitted with the paddle atachment cream together butter, brown sugar, granulated sugar and salt until pale and fluffy about 3 minutes.

Step 3

Stir in half and half. Add in flour and mix until just combined.

Step 4

Drizzle in more half and half, 1/2 tbsp at a time, as neededto bring dough together. Mix in chocolate chips.

Step 5

Scoop out 1 tablespoon at a time and roll into balls. Place on parchment paper.

Step 6

Serve immediately or store cookie dough bites in an airtight container in refrigerator.
