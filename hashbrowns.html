Hashbrowns
Ingredient Amount
butter 1/4 stick
american cheese 7 slices
hashbrown box 1
vegitable oil enough to coat bottom of the pan
trail dust depends on size of pan

Step 1

Put hot water in the hashbrown box for 8 and a half minutes. Meanwhile coat the bottom of the pan vegatible oil. Heat the pan up to about halfway on the stovetop.

Step 2

Strain the water out of the hashbrowns. then spread them evenly across the preheated pan. Cook them for about 5 minutes or until you can smell them cooking. Meanwhile start the broiler.

Step 3

Put the pan in the oven for 5 minutes. Then rotate it 180 degrees and leave it like that for 2.5 minutes. Don't let it burn.

Step 4

Turn off the broiler. Put 5 equally sliced chips of butter (this is all of it) on the pan. Then 5 slices of cheese. Put on 3 shakes of trail dust on. Then put the pan back in the oven until the cheese is melted.

Step 5

Then put it on a plate. Fold it in half so the cheese from the last step is on the inside. Then put the last 2 slices on top, and microwave it for 15 second.
